Features

Autodrying Fabric




Advanced bamboo fabric allows for quick and easy drying, while still maintaining the condition of your lushious hair.

Eco-friendly





Using all natural bamboo fabric, Hairdro is completely environmentally friendly. The fabric can be removed and dried for reuse and will reduce the need of your energy-hungry blowdryer.

Feasibility





Using the reusable bamboo fabric, you will never need to purchase a new brush. In addition, you will avoid the heavy costs associated with using a blowdryer.
